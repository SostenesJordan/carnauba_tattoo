Common Questions

FAQ

Does it hurt?

Pain varies depending on the person and the area of the body. Most clients describe it as tolerable, and breaks are always allowed if needed.

How long does a session last?

On average, a session lasts 6 to 8 hours, depending on the project.

How does the deposit work?

A $200 deposit secures your appointment, covers artwork creation, and acts as a cancellation fee. The amount is deducted from the final tattoo price.

Can I reschedule or cancel my appointment?

Yes. Rescheduling is allowed with at least 72 hours notice. In case of late cancellation or no-show, the deposit is non-refundable.

How do I take care of my tattoo afterward?

You'll receive detailed aftercare instructions. In general: keep it clean, apply the recommended ointment, and avoid sun, pools, or beaches until fully healed.

Healing Process

Aftercare

For the best healing results, follow these steps:

1. Saniderm Film

  • At the end of your session, your tattoo will be covered with Saniderm, a professional healing film.
  • It protects the tattoo from bacteria and friction.
  • Keep the film on for 4 full days.
  • To remove it, do so during a shower with warm water, carefully peeling it off.

2. Aquaphor Ointment

  • After removing Saniderm, apply a thin layer of Aquaphor about 3 times a day for the next few days.
  • This keeps the tattoo hydrated and helps the healing process.

3. General Care

  • Wash gently with neutral soap and cold water.
  • Avoid scratching, peeling scabs, or exposing the tattoo to direct sunlight.
  • Do not swim or use saunas/pools until the tattoo is fully healed.

Proper aftercare ensures your tattoo maintains its detail, contrast, and quality for years to come.
